HOW TECHNOLOGY CHANGED THE WORLD

It is crazy how technology evolved and how technology has helped us in our everyday lives from people waiting days for their photo to be released to just a click and a second you will see a high quality pictures you can even edit it nowadays with filters. And today more than ever, During the Covid-19 Pandemic, Technology has played a vital role in making our lives easier. From online delivery to working from home, technology is the backbone of everything. Every other industry is trying it’s best to make full use of technological resources and provide its services at this time of the pandemic.Without technology, we would not have been able to handle the situations as efficiently as we did during this crisis. Therefore, it is because of technology that the world has changed and is constantly changing.

The impact of technology in our daily lives is far more widespread than we actually think. It has changed our ways to communicate, socialize, and entertain ourselves and it is growing at a rapid pace. From the smartphone revolution to clean energy, technology has had an impact on our lives in almost every way. The delivery services that we use today, the online payment, the internet that we use every day is all part of the technology that has made our lives easier. Technology is growing at a rapid pace and it has had its positive impacts on society. It has changed the way we access resources. It has changed our ways to learn new things. Now, people tend to rely on it for everything.

IMPACT OF INFORMATON TECHNOLOGY ON BUSINESSES

Information Technology has dramatically transformed business. It helps businesses analyze and plan strategies according to their needs. The sheer accuracy of IT has helped businesses optimize its profits. they even adapted new measures on order for some buisnesses to survive in the middle of the pandemic

AUTOMATION SETTING PROTECTION
Processes like billing, collecting data, etc are easily automated. The automation of business has helped in reducing costs and saving time. This makes businesses more efficient and profitable.
During this time of the pandemic working remotely has been very beneficial as employees can work remotely from their home while not being physically present at the company’s official workplace.
Every company has a gigantic database that consists of business and client data. These are very important to be stored for future reference and use, but the physical data storage might not be enough to store such a vast amount of data. Hence, a virtual database or a cloud service is used which can store an infinite amount of data.

overall, In this era where technology is everything, companies need to use advanced technologies to provide better services. Adapting to new, faster, and safer technologies will be fruitful to the business in the long run. Technology has therefore changed and improved our living standards a lot by providing benefits in business, communication, transportation, and entertainment.
